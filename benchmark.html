The table below provides information about the 185 pairs of sequences of our benchmark. The column labeled Gene includes the name of the gene in Homo sapiens. HSapSize and HmlgSize contains, respectively, the size of the Homo sapiens sequence and its corresponding homologous, including extra 1000 bases before and after the gene sequence. Homologous indicates the specie of the used homologous sequence. RealEx is the amount of annotated exons in each sequence (observe that both sequences have the same number of exons). BSize and CSize are the amounts of potential exons generated by the GENSCAN for the Homo sapiens and the homologous sequences, respectively. PredEx is the number of predicted exons by GENEPREDICTOR. Finally, CorrExB and CorrExC are the amounts of real exons that were correctly predicted by GENEPREDICTOR in Homo sapiens and in the homologous sequences, respectively.
